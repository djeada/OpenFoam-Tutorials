# OpenFOAM-tutorials

<h1>CFD</h1>
Solving sets of governing equations using computer.
You don't have to code unless you have a novel perturbation model.
Comerical and open source
Parallel Processing

Conservation Laws:
* Conservation of Mass
* Conservation of Momentum
* Conservation of Energy

Conservation of Mass -> Continuity Equation
Conservation of Momentum -> Navier-Stokes Equation
Conservation of Energy -> Energy Equation
